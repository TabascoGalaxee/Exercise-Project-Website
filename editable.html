Hamstring Stretches

            How to do Hamstring Stretches

        https://www.mayoclinic.org/healthy-lifestyle/fitness/in-depth/stretching/art-20546848

1) Sit on the floor with one leg straight and the other bent, sole touching inner thigh.
2) Keep your back straight and chest lifted.
3) Lean forward from your hips, reaching towards your toes.
4) Hold the stretch for 15-30 seconds while breathing deeply.
5) Switch legs and repeat.
6) Repeat 2-3 times per week for flexibility improvement.
